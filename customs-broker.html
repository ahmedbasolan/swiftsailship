Required Documents

Customs Documentation Checklist

Essential documents needed for smooth customs clearance in the UAE. We help you prepare everything correctly.

Import Documents

  • Commercial Invoice: Original invoice with complete product details and values
  • Packing List: Detailed list of all packages with weights and dimensions
  • Bill of Lading / Airway Bill: Transport document from carrier
  • Certificate of Origin: Document certifying country of manufacture
  • Import License: If required for restricted goods
  • Insurance Certificate: Proof of cargo insurance coverage

Export Documents

  • Export Declaration: Customs export declaration form
  • Commercial Invoice: Seller's invoice for the exported goods
  • Packing List: Complete details of packaged goods
  • Certificate of Origin: UAE Chamber of Commerce certified document
  • Export License: For controlled or restricted items
  • Destination Requirements: Any additional documents required by importing country
