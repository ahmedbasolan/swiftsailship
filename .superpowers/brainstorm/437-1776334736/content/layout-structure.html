Which homepage structure fits best?

All three use the Editorial Prestige aesthetic (cream + charcoal + gold). The difference is how they guide the visitor through the journey.

Services

A — Classic Narrative

Hero → Stats → Services (4-grid) → Why Us → CTA. Familiar flow, easy to scan, proven conversion pattern.

B — Editorial Split

Split hero → Services (4-col) → About strip → How It Works → CTA. Tells the story while showcasing services early.

C — Services-First + Inline Quote

Hero → Trust bar → Featured services (asymmetric) → Embedded mini-quote form. Maximizes conversion without a separate page visit.
