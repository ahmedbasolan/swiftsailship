Documentation

Essential Documents

Proper documentation is crucial for smooth customs clearance. We guide you through preparing all necessary paperwork for your shipment.

For Imports

  • Commercial Invoice: Detailed invoice with goods description, value, and terms
  • Packing List: Complete list of all packages with weights and dimensions
  • Bill of Lading: Transport contract between shipper and carrier
  • Certificate of Origin: Document certifying the country where goods were produced
  • Trade License: Importer's valid trade license copy

For Exports

  • Commercial Invoice: Detailed invoice with export terms and pricing
  • Packing List: Comprehensive packing details for export verification
  • Bill of Lading: Export transport documentation
  • Export Declaration: Official export documentation
  • Trade License: Exporter's valid trade license copy

FAQs

Common Questions

Get answers to frequently asked questions about our shipping process and services.

How long does the entire shipping process take?
Total transit time varies by destination. Typically, sea freight from Asia to UAE takes 10-20 days, while Europe to UAE takes 12-25 days. Add 2-5 days for customs clearance and local delivery.
What documents do I need for customs clearance?
Essential documents include commercial invoice, packing list, bill of lading, certificate of origin, and your trade license. Additional documents may be required based on cargo type and origin.
Can you handle hazardous materials?
Yes, we handle hazardous materials with proper certification and compliance. Additional documentation and handling procedures apply. Contact us for specific requirements.
Do you provide cargo insurance?
Yes, we offer comprehensive cargo insurance options to protect your shipment against loss, damage, or delays during transit. Coverage can be customized based on your needs.
How do I track my shipment?
We provide real-time tracking through our online portal and regular updates via email or WhatsApp. You'll receive notifications at key milestones throughout the shipping process.
